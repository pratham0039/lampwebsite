Bunty Tomar

Bunty Tomar is a PhD candidate at the Laboratory for Advanced Manufacturing and Processing (LAMP). Tomar received his Master of Technology (MTech) in Materials Science and Engineering (MSE) from the National Institute of Advanced Manufacturing Technology (NIAMT) Ranchi (Formerly NIFFT), India, in 2020 in the area of metal manufacturing. He received his Bachelor of Technology (BTech) from Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam University Lucknow, India, from the Department of Mechanical Engineering in 2017. In particular, the focus of his PhD is on the Wire Arc Additive Manufacturing of functionally graded materials (FGMs). He is currently working on FGMs based on Copper, Titanium, Chromium, and various grades of steels.
